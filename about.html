Standout Features

Explore the key features that make our platform the perfect tool for students to manage school life effortlessly.

Collaborative Information Sharing

Students can easily share subject-specific notes, class schedules, and homework updates. Every student has access to the latest information from their peers, ensuring everyone stays on track.

Homework Assistance

Students can view, share, and collaborate on assignments directly through the app. Whether it's seeking help or offering solutions, the homework feature encourages teamwork.

Simplified Attendance Management

Track attendance with ease. Students can mark their attendance via the app, providing real-time updates for the school. A personalized dashboard gives a clear overview of attendance statistics.

Seamless Communication

Students can communicate and collaborate using the in-app chat feature. Whether it's discussing homework or sharing ideas, our chat feature helps solve problems quickly.

User-Centric Design

Enjoy a beautifully designed interface that's easy to navigate. Our app is crafted to offer a smooth and intuitive experience, making every interaction effortless.

News Feed (Coming Soon)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SchoolHub?

SchoolHub is an all-in-one app designed for students to manage attendance, homework, schedules, and more, while fostering a collaborative environment.

Can I track my attendance using SchoolHub?

Yes, students can mark their daily attendance, and the app tracks their overall attendance to help ensure they meet the minimum requirement.

How does the homework sharing feature work?

Students can share and view homework assignments, making it easier to stay on top of tasks and collaborate with classmates.

Is there a chat feature for students?

Yes, SchoolHub includes a chat feature where students can communicate and solve academic problems together in real-time.

Can I customize my profile?

Absolutely! Students can customize their profiles with a profile picture and view their attendance, homework, and class-related activities.

What are the upcoming features of SchoolHub?

In the near future, we will add a news feed feature for school announcements, events, and activities, creating a sense of community within the app.
